Frequently Asked Questions

You have a lot of questions? We are here to answer.

Is Noble bank a registered bank?

Yes. We have a microfinance banking license from the Central Bank Of Nigeria. Our Registration Number is RC 123456.

What should I do if I need help?

The easiest way to reach us is to tap MORE on the Noble App, then proceed to tap CHAT WITH US. You can also send a mail to help@Noble.com or call 012 345 678 between the hours of 8am and 5pm on weekdays.

What fees do you charge?

Sign Up with your BVN and get 20 free transactions every month, and #10 service charge on every additional transaction.

How does Noble bank make money?

We make money from commision on airtime sales/purchases, and other service fees paid when you shop with the Noble Card.

What kind of account will I get?

Sign Up with your Personal details, BVN, and a snapshot of any of your "Utility Bill" to get a TIER 1 account with a maximum daily transaction of 100 000, and a maximum balance of 500 000.

Is my money safe with Noble bank?

YES. Your money is safe with us, as we secure all accounts with the same high-security encryption used by regular banks.
